Vivian Chen


I am pursuing my MSc in Urban Informatics at New York University's Center for Urban Science and Progress, with anticipated graduation in July 2020. My goal is to strengthen my geo- and data- visualization and analytical skills to create positive change and long-term solutions for cities around the world, beginning with my home, New York City. My interests include understanding urban infrastructure patterns, promoting accessibility to public transportation, and learning what makes cities fail and succeed.

My past experiences include working as a transportation intern at the NYC Eonomic Development Corporation within the Planning Division, a college aide at the NYC Department of Transportation in the Asset Management Unit, and a teaching assistant at Barnard College for an Intro to GIS course. I believe GIS and data visualization are very applicable tools that can strengthen any research, and hope to incorporate my skills to any work that I do.

To get in contact with me, please email me at vivianc206@gmail.com.
