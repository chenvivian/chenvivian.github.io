Vivian Chen


I am pursuing my MSc in Urban Informatics at New York University's Center for Urban Science and Progress, with anticipated graduation in August 2020. My goal is to strengthen my geo- and data- visualization and analytical skills to create positive change and long-term solutions for cities around the world, beginning with my home, New York City. My interests include understanding urban infrastructure patterns, promoting accessibility to public transportation, and learning what makes cities fail and succeed.

Currently, I work at the NYC Department of Transportation within the Asset Management Unit to update and create maps and databases. A teacher assistant at Barnard College for an Intro to GIS course, I aim to share my knowledge and experience in GIS with undergraduate students. I believe GIS and data visualization are very applicable tools that can strengthen any research.

To get in contact with me, please email me at vivianc206@gmail.com.
